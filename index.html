I felt happy and even happier when I passed the school's entrance exam. I am thrilled to have come this far.

The reason I joined Alt_school Africa was to achieve my current goal which is to establish a strong and practical foundation in coding and software engineering with a hope to get internships. Joining Alt_School Africa made me feel like I made a bold step and a good progress towards my goal. And My goal doesn't end at Alt_school Africa. I still have plans of getting a B.Sc. and eventually a master's in Computer Science. As I look towards the future, I am filled with a sense of purpose and determination. The journey ahead may seem challenging, but I am prepared to face it head-on with determination and a relentless drive for success.
